About Us

  • About Us

Established in 2023, we are a diverse team passionate about technology, linguistics, language translation, analytics, and AI. Our collective expertise spans multiple domains, and our mission is clear: to leverage our skills for the betterment of Chinland and the Chin Community.

Through innovative projects like the Falam Translator and comprehensive training initiatives, we strive to empower our community and drive positive change.

Our mission is

to leverage expertise in Data Science, Machine Learning, Analytics, and Linguistics to benefit Chinland and the Chin Community.

  • Awareness: Raise public awareness about new technologies, their use cases, benefits, and risks.
  • Innovation: Develop projects crucial to the Chin community through creativity and forward-thinking.
  • Training: Conduct training on new technologies, fields, Machine Learning, and AI to empower individuals.
